Saying farewell to eco3P.com

World's First Carbon Neutral Marketplace

When we launched eco3P (pronounced "eco-three-pee"), our mission was to challenge the status quo and rethink the traditional ecommerce business model. While a lot of the ideas we developed could work inside the eco3P framework, we believe we can have a larger impact if we move beyond its scope. To allow us to focus on our broader vision, we are saying goodbye to eco3P.

eco3P.com was sunsetted on January 1, 2017.

- The eco3P team
